5 Reasons to Wash Your Fleet

1. Less Wear and Tear

A neat and clean fleet will always last a lot longer than a dirty one.

2. Less Chance of Breakdown

All parts of a vehicle function better when clean. Road salt, used to melt ice and snow, is a significant factor in vehicle corrosion.

3. Create Brand Loyalty

Your fleet is the most visible part of your brand. Clean vehicles are a good way to advertise your business.

4. Lower Operational Expenses

A vehicle sitting in the garage increases operating cost. Keeping your fleet clean will help prevent some breakdowns.

5. Attract & Retain Drivers

Well maintained fleet attracts drivers and helps retain drivers. Managers can use the overall condition and maintenance of the fleet to attract drivers.
